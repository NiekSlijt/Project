About us

We are a 5 man team that worked on this site for a school project. Together we came with the idea to make a site about New York City. We wanted to make this site so that people know more about the city and also have a route to walk to explore points of interest in the city. Our goal was to lead people to a place that they normally would walk by and think wow this is a nice building or statue and give them information about that building or statue.
